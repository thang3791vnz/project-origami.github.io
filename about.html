About US

In the years 2004-2005, when we were still just girls, schoolboys, students still going to school, going to graduate school, young and sharing the same passion, a strong passion. for Origami. In the past, we formed an association group called Vietnam Origami Group with the desire to connect the hearts of the members who love paper folding from all over Vietnam together and continue. keep blowing your passion about Origami to other young people across the S-shaped strip of land.
In the early days, we exchanged passionately about our experiences, techniques in paper folding, and introduced each other about new compositions, though still in its infancy. So far, we have organized many exhibitions, workshops on introduction, teaching about Origami in schools, centers, cultural houses and cultural exchange events in big cities. small nationwide. Talented members also gradually affirmed their names, were introduced and invited to attend international exhibitions through Origami to introduce Vietnamese people to friends all over the five continents ...
In Origami contains many profound philosophies of life, a magical worldview that is all formed by paper, brains, hands and burning passion. And most of all, VPFA is also a place where we have opened a big embrace to connect with friends from all over the world, not only in Vietnam but also around the world. We, Vietnam Paper Folding Association hope to contribute a small part of our efforts to the historical flow of modern Origami.
