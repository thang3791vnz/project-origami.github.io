Aquatic animals

The ocean accounts for 70% of the earth's surface area. We have an infinite variety of marine life. Let's take a look at Origami works on this vast topic.
Above the banner is an image of a swordfish from designer Marlin - Takuro Kashiwamura. Let's start to admire some great origami works

1. Hermit Crab

https://www.flickr.com/photos/marianozavala-origami/35895916075/in/dateposted/

The artwork Hermit Crab by Satoshi Kamiya, it is made up of two pieces of paper,
with the parameter of a two tissue paper 72 x 72 cm.
