Dog

Dogs are an animal that is very close to humans so it is not surprising that ogami appeared so many pictures of dogs.
Puppies are considered as symbols of loyalty in Japan. The artists have attempted to photograph using single pieces of paper to fold various types of dogs.
We have collected a lot of Origami models, but there are many duplicates so we have selected the best ones to give to you.

1. Doberman

https://www.flickr.com/photos/143234267@N08/25654991768/

Doberman is the name of a breed that can be kept for house keeping, guard or profession.
Its complete and exact name is Dobermann Pinscher.

Doberman Designed by J.W Park

4. Finnish Lapphund

https://www.flickr.com/photos/126871950@N04/29448134695/

The Finnish Lapphund is a hardy, easy going, medium-size breed of Spitz type. Traditionally it has been used for herding reindeer.
Although it is one of the most popular dog breeds in its native country, Finland, it is not very numerous outside of the Nordic countries.

Designed by Seth Friedman - Folded by Ivan Svatko
One uncut 35x35cm square of mulberry.

5. Pug

https://www.flickr.com/photos/53248898@N07/41388733651/

The pug is a breed of dog with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail.
The breed has a fine, glossy coat that comes in a variety of colours, most often fawn or black.

Designed by Yoo Tae Yong - Folded by Adri 79
Paper Kraft + Rice 35x35 cm - 107 steps
Book : Origami Works of Yoo Tae Yong
