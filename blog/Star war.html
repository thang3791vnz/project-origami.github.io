Star War

After Disney's acquisition of Lucasfilm, only the later one remained canon. Three live-action Star Wars series will be released on Disney+. The first, The Mandalorian, premiered on November 12, 2019. Star Wars won the 2020 Webby Award for Television & Film in the category Social.
Let's see the characters in Star Wars through the art of Origami.

1. Resistance X Wing

https://www.flickr.com/photos/66587743@N04/28712361351/

Designed by Karol Kafarski - Folded by Michael Novio
Took about 3 hours or so to fold.
Paper: 35cm square of Kami.

3. BB-8

https://www.flickr.com/photos/starwarigami/32811339756/

Designed - Folded by Martin Hunt
One sheet of 10" paper back foil cut into quarters lengthwise and widthwise to make 16 equal squares, folded to form 4 different types of module.
Locking folds have been improved for neatness and security and ease of assembly, and head proportions modifed to sit more snugly on the ball.
