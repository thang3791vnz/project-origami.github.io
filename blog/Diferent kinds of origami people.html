Diferent kinds of origami people

Until now, artisans often re-sketched images of people with pictures. There are also many people who use sculptural artwork to sketch images of people. So is Origami artisans use paper material to create works of art depicting human activities. Here are a few of the representative pieces we have collected.

1. A Bird on Hand

https://www.flickr.com/photos/-adri79-/16057288759/

A Bird on Hand designed by Watanabe Dai - Folded by Adriano Davanzo.
Paper rice + banana 40x40 cm - 129 steps.

2. Iron Man

https://www.flickr.com/photos/128874294@N04/44104417384/

Iron Man designed by Yoo Tae Yong - Folded by J.W Park.
Probably the best version of this model.

3. Ribbon dance

https://www.flickr.com/photos/128874294@N04/44104417384/

Ribbon dance is Series of models created for the exhibition "Transformed reality" in -EMOZ- Zaragoza-Spain.
Banana fiber paper 27 * 27 cm.

4. Archangel St Michael

https://www.flickr.com/photos/84138735@N00/29468847807/in/dateposted/

Archangel St Michael designed and folded by Tran Trung Hieu.
Beautiful art form Viet Nam
